## What is CNC Metal Engraving?

Metal engraving involves etching text, numbers, logos, or intricate designs into the surface of metal using a high-speed, computer-controlled router or engraver. Unlike chemical etching or simple laser marking, CNC routing physically removes material, resulting in a deep, durable, and highly visible engraving that can withstand harsh environments.
