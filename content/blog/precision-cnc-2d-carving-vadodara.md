## What is CNC 2D Carving?

Unlike 3D carving which plays with depth and sculptural reliefs, 2D carving (often referred to as 2D cutting or routing) focuses on cutting precise shapes, patterns, and outlines into flat sheet materials. Our state-of-the-art CNC routers follow digital vectors to cut right through the material or engrave surface-level patterns with millimeter accuracy.
