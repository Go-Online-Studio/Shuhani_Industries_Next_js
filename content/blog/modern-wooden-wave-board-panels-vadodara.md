## The Magic of 3D Wave Boards

Wooden wave boards are engineered MDF or solid wood panels that have been CNC-machined to feature continuous, flowing, wave-like patterns. When installed side-by-side on a wall, the pattern aligns perfectly, creating a seamless, sculptural surface that plays beautifully with lighting and shadows.

### Where to Use Wave Board Panels?
