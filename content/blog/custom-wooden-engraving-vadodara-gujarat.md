## What is CNC Wooden Engraving?

Unlike deep 3D carving, wooden engraving focuses on etching designs, text, or logos into the surface of the wood. Using advanced CNC technology, we can engrave with a level of detail and consistency that manual methods simply cannot match. From fine, elegant text to complex vector graphics, our machines handle it all with ease.
